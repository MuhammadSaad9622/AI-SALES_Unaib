AI Sales Assistant Extension

Installation & Usage Instructions

Overview

The AI Sales Assistant Chrome Extension enhances your sales calls by providing real-time AI-powered suggestions and live transcription. It works with popular meeting platforms like Google Meet, Zoom, and Microsoft Teams.

Real-time AI Suggestions

Get contextual sales tips and responses during your calls to help close deals more effectively.

Live Transcription

Automatic speech-to-text conversion of your sales conversations for real-time reference.

Multi-platform Support

Works seamlessly with Google Meet, Zoom, and Microsoft Teams for all your virtual meetings.

Installation Instructions

Google Chrome
Microsoft Edge
Mozilla Firefox

  1. Download the Extension

    If you haven't already, download the extension ZIP file from your AI Sales Assistant dashboard.

    Download Extension

  2. Extract the ZIP File

    Right-click the downloaded ZIP file and select "Extract All..." (Windows) or double-click the file (Mac) to extract its contents to a folder.

    Make note of where you extract the files. You'll need this location in the next step.

  3. Open Chrome Extensions Page

    Open Google Chrome and navigate to chrome://extensions/ by typing it in the address bar.

  4. Enable Developer Mode

    Toggle on "Developer mode" using the switch in the top-right corner of the extensions page.

  5. Load the Extension

    Click the "Load unpacked" button that appears after enabling Developer mode. Navigate to the folder where you extracted the extension files and select it.

  6. Verify Installation

    The AI Sales Assistant extension should now appear in your extensions list. You should also see the extension icon in your browser toolbar.

    If you don't see the icon, click the puzzle piece icon in your toolbar and pin the AI Sales Assistant extension.

Using the Extension

  1. Sign In

    Click the AI Sales Assistant icon in your browser toolbar. Sign in with your AI Sales Assistant account credentials.

  2. Join a Meeting

    Start or join a meeting in Google Meet, Zoom, or Microsoft Teams as you normally would.

  3. Start the Assistant

    Click the AI Sales Assistant icon in your browser toolbar and press the "Start Call" button. Grant microphone permissions if prompted.

    You can also enable "Auto-start on meeting join" in the extension settings to automatically activate the assistant when you join a meeting.

  4. Use AI Suggestions

    During your call, the extension will display a floating panel with real-time AI suggestions based on the conversation. Click on a suggestion to copy it to your clipboard.

  5. View Live Transcription

    The extension will also display a floating panel with the live transcription of the conversation. This can help you keep track of important points discussed.

  6. End Recording

    When your call is finished, click the AI Sales Assistant icon and press the "End Call" button to stop recording and processing.

    All call data will be securely stored in your AI Sales Assistant account for later review.

Troubleshooting

Extension Not Appearing

If the extension doesn't appear in your browser after installation:

  • Make sure you've enabled Developer mode in your browser's extensions page
  • Try reloading the extensions page
  • Restart your browser
  • Re-download and extract the extension files, then try loading it again

Microphone Access Issues

If the extension can't access your microphone:

  • Make sure you've granted microphone permissions to the extension
  • Check your browser's site settings to ensure microphone access is allowed
  • Make sure no other application is using your microphone exclusively
  • Try using a different microphone if available

No Suggestions Appearing

If you're not seeing any AI suggestions during your call:

  • Verify that you're signed in to your AI Sales Assistant account
  • Check that your account is active and has the necessary permissions
  • Ensure your microphone is working properly and picking up audio
  • Try speaking more clearly and at a normal volume
  • Check your internet connection, as suggestions require online processing

Privacy & Security

The AI Sales Assistant extension is designed with your privacy and security in mind:

  • All audio processing is done through your secure AI Sales Assistant account
  • The extension only activates during meetings when you explicitly start recording
  • No audio is recorded without your permission
  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • The extension UI elements are never visible when sharing your screen
  • You can delete recorded call data at any time from your AI Sales Assistant dashboard

Important: Always inform all participants that the call is being recorded and transcribed. This is not only a courtesy but may be legally required in some jurisdictions.
